Our positions

Senior Consultant - Finance & Accounting

Craftsman is seeking an experienced

Requirements

  • A smart person
  • A driven person
  • A team player
  • Prior headhunting experience is not a must
  • Demonstrated experience in professional services or sales is a definite plus
  • Major in Finance, Accounting or Economics is preferred

Experience building production applications with Metronic would be good to have as well.

Responsibilities

Business Development and Client Relationship Management

  • Follow latest hiring market trends about finance and accounting area in China;
  • Find recruitment needs from existing and potential clients;
  • Independently client visit to introduce service, discuss candidates profiles, and negotiate contracts, etc;
  • Regularly interact and build up good rapport with clients.

Contingent Search Project Management

  • Discuss clients’ recruitment needs and assist in drafting Job Descriptions;
  • Headhunt qualified candidates by mapping market, cold-calling, using personal networks and database;
  • Screen CVs and conduct face-to-face, telephone or video interviews with potential candidates;
  • Write candidates recommendation reports and discuss profiles with clients;
  • Arrange interview process for candidates with clients and handle issues and problems from both sides in the whole process;
  • Offer negotiation, reference check and on board assistance;
  • Follow up status and feedbacks about both clients and candidates after on boarding.

Professional Advisory

  • Provide advisory to clients regarding the market trends, salary guide and recruiting skills, including deliver reports regarding professional suggestions on tough recruitment cases; analysis report about candidates’ attitude, etc;
  • Provide career development advisory to candidates.

Qualified candidates, send resume and salary requirements with form in the sidebar or contact: enquires@craftsmanasia.com.
