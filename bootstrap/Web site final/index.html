Our Mission

Craftsman’s mission is to provide the most efficient service to meet the needs of both our clients and candidates. We try to achieve the goal through functional specialisation and consultative approach, which is supported by our Big Data Engine CRM+ .

Key Differentiators

Highly functional specialised recruitment firm with industry matrix; Team performance oriented business model; A SMART matching and assessment data platform.

Specialisation

Craftsman Financial Market helps organizations achieve their business strategies by attracting, developing, and retaining best-in-class talent. As the financial industry addresses increased regulatory pressures, new calls for transparency, and the necessity to balance planned growth with shrinking margins, agile, strategic leaders who can lead through change are essential. Today’s financial services talent must be nimble and possess insightful strategic skills.

Craftsman Information and Technology Sector focuses on helping companies attract, develop, and retain best-in-class leaders. We understand the shifting needs for brilliant technical talent as well as the importance of making sure your business has the right people to lead it through change. We offer a full range of solutions designed to meet systems and devices talent needs.

Management Consultancy focuses on helping clients attract, develop, and retain best-in-class consulting. Professional services firms of all types are increasingly required by their clients to be global in delivery capability, possess broad functional expertise, and be deep knowledge experts in their particular industry.
