Whats the Craft?

Craftsman is a partnership recruitment firm founded in Hong Kong by a group of international professionals. The “Crafts” of Craftsman is to locate and service top tier professional, providing a seamless link between professionals and enterprises.

What is Our Mission?

Equipped with good knowledge of specialized industries and functions, our consultants are able to provide a tailor-made job matching and career counselling service to various candidates.

Even if you are not an active candidate for a job seeker, you will have easy access to the first-hand market information in your particular functions by following our company page on LinkedIn.

Our mission is to minimize the information gap between you and the market, and to provide the exciting opportunity for you to achieve your career ambition.

What is the difference?

  • Consultant - Equipped with good knowledge of specialized industries and functions, our consultants managed both candidates and clients sides to facilitate communications.
  • Service - Our functional specialization enables us with a prompt and precise target search.
  • Team - With low turnover rate and efficient teamwork, we are able to minimize the communication cost and always put the clients' benefits at first priority.
  • Big Data – With the most advanced recruitment big data engines to generate the best matching link, supported by Wechat follow through function and CRM Client management tools.
